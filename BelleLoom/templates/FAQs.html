  FAQs



Q: What is BelleLoom?

A: BelleLoom is an online fashion store that brings you a wide range of clothing and accessories. What sets us apart is that most of our products are carefully made by hand, adding a special touch and uniqueness to each item. We believe in supporting skilled artisans and showcasing their craftsmanship..

Q: What sizes are available for women's clothing?

A: We offer a range of sizes to cater to different body types. You can find size guides on the product pages to help you choose the right fit.

Q: Can I return or exchange items if they don't meet my expectations?

A: Our return and exchange policy applies to all products, including women's clothing and accessories. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange items within 30 days. Please review our Returns & Exchanges page for detailed instructions.

Q: Are there specific care instructions for sarees and clothing items?

A: We provide care instructions for our clothing and sarees on the product pages. It's important to follow these guidelines to ensure the longevity of your items.

Q: How can I track the delivery of my order?

A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and location of your package.

Q: What is the estimated delivery time for orders?

A: Delivery times may vary based on your location and chosen shipping method. We strive to process and ship orders promptly. You can find estimated delivery times on our Shipping & Delivery page.

Q: Can I contact customer support if I have questions or need assistance?

A: Absolutely! If you have any questions or need assistance with your order, you can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website or by emailing shubhnishv01@gmail.com
